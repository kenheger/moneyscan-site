AI Era Career Tools
♾️ AI Literacy Assessment
12-question interactive quiz designed to measure not just familiarity, but your ability to use AI strategically for career advantage and wealth building.
Take the Assessment
Rate each statement from 0–3 (0 = Never / Strongly Disagree, 3 = Always / Strongly Agree):

I regularly use AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.) to help me work faster or smarter.
I know how to write effective prompts that get useful, specific results instead of generic answers.
I can critically evaluate AI output for accuracy, bias, or hallucinations.
I use AI to research or analyze topics related to my career or finances.
I understand the difference between using AI as a tool versus becoming overly dependent on it.
I have experimented with AI to automate repetitive tasks in my job or side projects.
I actively look for ways AI can increase my earning power (e.g., new services, faster delivery, better quality).
I track how AI is changing jobs and opportunities in my industry or local area.
I combine my existing domain knowledge/skills with AI tools to create unique value.
I have a basic understanding of AI’s limitations (what it’s good at and what it still struggles with).
I regularly practice using AI for wealth-building activities (researching investments, analyzing side hustle ideas, optimizing budgets, etc.).
I view AI as a collaborator that can help me build surplus faster rather than a threat to my career.

Scoring

0–12 points: Beginner – Focus on foundational prompting and daily use.
13–24 points: Intermediate – Start experimenting with automation and career applications.
25–36 points: Advanced – Look for high-leverage opportunities to turn AI into real surplus.
